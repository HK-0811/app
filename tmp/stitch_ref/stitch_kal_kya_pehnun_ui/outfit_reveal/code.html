Why this works

This ensemble leverages Tonal Contrast to create depth without visual noise. The juxtaposition of the structured charcoal denim against the fluid cream Pima tee creates a silhouette that is both grounded and airy.

Color Theory

The palette follows the 60-30-10 rule: 60% neutral cream, 30% deep charcoal, and 10% metallic gold accents. This ensures a balanced, high-end editorial feel.
